Recipai – AI Cooking Assistant

Personal PM Case Study

Overview

Recipai is an AI-powered cooking assistant designed to help users plan meals and personalize their cooking experience.

This case study highlights my individual product thinking and contributions within a team-based project.

Problem

  • Users struggle to decide what to cook on a daily basis
  • Managing dietary restrictions is challenging
  • Recipes and grocery planning feel disconnected

Users

  • Busy students and young professionals
  • Users with dietary restrictions

Goals

  • Reduce decision fatigue when choosing meals
  • Enable personalized recipe recommendations
  • Simplify grocery planning

Solution

  • AI Chat Assistant for recipe discovery and guidance
  • Personalization settings for dietary preferences
  • Smart shopping lists to streamline grocery preparation

Trade-offs & Constraints

Due to time and scope constraints, advanced features such as nutrition tracking were deprioritized in favor of delivering a stable and usable MVP.

Outcome & Learnings

This project strengthened my ability to translate user needs into product decisions, balance scope and feasibility, and communicate product value clearly.
